About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the Ugandan team behind HopeForAll Foundation

Our Story

HopeForAll Foundation was established in 2010 with a vision to create sustainable change in underprivileged communities across Western Uganda. What started as a small group of local volunteers has grown into a respected organization with operations in 5 districts.

Our founders, Dr. Grace Nansamba and Mr. Emmanuel Tumwine, witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by communities without access to basic necessities like education, healthcare, and clean water. They believed that every Ugandan deserves the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their circumstances.

Over the years, we've expanded our programs to address the most pressing needs of the communities we serve, always with a focus on sustainable solutions that empower local residents to become agents of change in their own communities.

Our Vision

A Uganda where every individual has access to education, healthcare, and the resources needed to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities in Western Uganda by providing access to education, healthcare, and sustainable resources, fostering self-reliance and positive change.

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, sustainability, and collaboration guide everything we do at HopeForAll Foundation.
