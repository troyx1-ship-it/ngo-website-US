Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer in Uganda?

We have various volunteering opportunities available for both local and international volunteers. Fill out our volunteer form and we'll contact you with available positions.

Where does my donation go?

85% of donations go directly to our programs in Western Uganda, with 15% covering essential administrative and operational costs.

Can I visit your projects in Uganda?

Yes, we organize donor visits to our project sites in Western Uganda. Contact us to schedule a visit.
